1. Introduction

Welcome to Vanta ("we," "our," or "us").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Vanta, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

a. Information You Provide

  • Account information (e.g. email, username)
  • Profile details (if provided)
  • Content you upload (e.g. tattoo images, captions)
  • Actions within the app (e.g. likes, saves, follows)

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Log data (IP address, timestamps, app interactions)
  • Usage data (features used, screens viewed, session duration)

c. Location Information

We may collect approximate location data based on your IP address or device settings.

This information is used to:

  • Personalize content and recommendations
  • Improve app functionality
  • Analyze usage trends

We do not collect precise GPS location unless explicitly stated and permitted by you.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the app

  • Enable core features (saving, uploading, sharing content)
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Monitor usage and analyze trends
  • Detect and prevent abuse or misuse

4. Analytics

We use analytics tools built on Supabase to understand how users interact with the app.

This may include:

  • Identifying bugs and performance issues

Analytics data is used to improve the app and is not sold to third parties.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information in the following cases:
